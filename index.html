Together as One


It was a night that Kortnee can only describe as “pure magic.”

I remember the moment I felt free to dream and think of great ways to propose to Kortnee. It was incredible. The floodgates opened up. All gears kicked in and I was running on all cylinders. Her parents had given me their permission. I knew the date I was going to propose, but not the plan. I didn't feel rushed to ask her. I wanted a great romantic proposal rather than just a simple one. (Either gets the job done when you know the girl you love is going to say yes, by the way.) However, I'm a creative guy and a hopeless romantic. I wanted something that would swoon her and make both of us giggly just thinking of it.

It was about a week after the dinner that I finally had figured it out. Sitting at a coffee shop, "doing work related things" I looked for the song first. About two hours into exploring various bands, strings, lyrics, and the like – I FOUND IT. The moment ‘Gone in Bloom and Bough’ by Caspian came on, I instantly started dreaming. The song is beautiful, so it was easy to think of the emotion I wanted Kortnee to feel, and what I wanted her to be doing; walking, reading, running. So off to work I went.

I was planning on doing all of this at a national forest just near our houses, when the forest department said I couldn't have Christmas lights or a generator on the property. Luckily, Kort and I have an amazing group of friends who have an abundant network of connections. My co-worker introduced me to a man with some land in Black Forest. It was scary how perfectly it worked out.

So, here's the good stuff.

Kortnee thought I was filming with a good friend I’ve been helping with on a video project. It was "basically done" but we needed to get a couple more shots to make it whole. We spent all day setting everything up. We had billion of Christmas lights (or at least the what it felt like), four speakers in the forest playing music, and plenty of party supplies to make the 'youth adventure-y' scene worth while. We were just wrapping up when, "uh-oh my tires flat". Leaving Kort to come to this mystery property. So she shows up and… the rest was pure magic.

She said "of course.” Now we’re blissfully on our way to marriage, in holy matrimony.
